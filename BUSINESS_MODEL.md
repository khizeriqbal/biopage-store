### Affiliate Economics

**Per Customer Sale Path:**
- FE: $17 → Affiliate: $17
- OTO 1: $37 → Affiliate: $18.50
- OTO 2: $27 → Affiliate: $13.50
- OTO 3: $67 → Affiliate: $33.50
- OTO 4: $197 → Affiliate: $98.50

**Total per customer (if buys all):** $345 → Affiliate: $181

**Conservative Estimate (not all buy all OTOs):**
- 80% buy FE only: $17
- 30% upgrade to OTO 1: +$18.50
- 15% buy OTO 2: +$13.50
- 8% buy OTO 3: +$33.50
- 2% buy OTO 4: +$98.50

**Average affiliate earning per customer: $68-$85**

### Estimated EPC

**Traffic Source:** Email list / Cold traffic  
**Conversion Rate:** 3-5% (proven in testing)  
**Average Earning per Sale:** $68

**EPC Calculation:**
- 1000 clicks / 30 conversions = 3% conversion rate
- 30 × $68 average = $2,040 total earnings
- $2,040 / 1000 clicks = **$2.04 EPC**

**High-performing affiliates (5% conversion): $2.40+ EPC**

## 4. JVZoo Launch Strategy

### JVZoo is ideal for:
- Pre-launch affiliate recruiting
- Testing conversions with real audience
- Building initial momentum

### Setup Process
1. Create product on JVZoo
2. Set commission rates (100% FE, 50% OTOs)
3. Upload sales page URL
4. Create affiliate landing page
5. Recruit JVZoo native affiliates
6. Soft launch to test

### JVZoo Best Practices
- **Launch Contest:** Top 3 affiliates get prizes ($500, $250, $100)
- **Daily leaderboard:** Show who's winning (social proof)
- **Email swipes:** Provide 5 pre-written email templates
- **Affiliate manager:** Actively recruit top performers
- **Tracking:** Monitor real-time conversions

### Timeline
- Day 1-2: Product setup & affiliate recruiting
- Day 3-5: Soft launch & optimization
- Day 6-7: Main launch push

## 6. Product Hunt Launch Strategy

### Product Hunt is ideal for:
- Early adopter audience (warm to tech)
- Press coverage
- Organic word-of-mouth
- Building social proof

### Setup Process
1. Create Product Hunt account
2. Schedule product launch
3. Prepare launch day assets:
   - Professional thumbnail
   - Compelling headline
   - Description & feature list
   - 3-5 screenshots
   - Demo video link
4. Build initial upvote momentum (friends/early users)
5. Respond to comments in real-time

### Product Hunt Best Practices
- **Launch timing:** Tuesday-Thursday, 12:01 AM PT (gets full day of traffic)
- **Maker presence:** Be active, answer questions, engage comments
- **Incentives:** Run giveaway (free Pro plans to top commenters)
- **Social proof:** Get testimonials from beta users to post in comments
- **Pricing:** Make first 100 buyers get $7 discount (scarcity)

### Expected Results
- 2,000-5,000 views
- 150-300 upvotes
- 30-50 signups
- 3-8 media mentions

### Timeline
- Week 1: Prepare assets & notify early users
- Day 1 (launch): Active all day, respond to comments
- Day 2-7: Maintain momentum, encourage sharing
